Add KML to the Map in three steps:

  1. Copy KML files on your computer to the "KML folder" in Maps on a Stick.
  2. Click "Choose File", open the "KML folder" on the USB drive and select the kml file.
  3. Click "Add Layer" and your KML data will be available to view on the map.
